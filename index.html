MANIFESTO

  1. Real freedom is created by liberating one's life from the decisions of another.
  2. Everyone should have the right to live and organise how they please, provided they do not exploit others.
  3. Communities should make their own decisions through voluntary agreement, where power stays at the local level.
  4. Society should work through meaningful co-operation, and only on projects relevant to each individual.
  5. We reject authoritarian systems, because no-one should have absolute power over others.
  6. A communal economy, where trade is free and co-operative, is more fair.
  7. Welfare is built through mutual aid, so the means of it should be delegated to those who receive it.
  8. Freedom of expression should be defended to encourage a diversity of ideas.
